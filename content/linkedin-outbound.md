# LinkedIn outbound — cold DM templates

**Target:** CISOs, CIOs, IT directors, Head of Information Security at EU mid-market companies (50-5000 employees). Prioritize Netherlands, then DACH, then Benelux+UK. Financial services and public-adjacent orgs (healthcare, transport, energy) are highest-value given NIS2/DORA exposure.

**Voice rules:**
- No "I hope this finds you well" openers. Straight to the hook.
- No em dashes. Commas and periods only.
- Always name a specific public source or number within the first two lines.
- Close with a CTA that takes 9 minutes or less.
- Personalize the first line to their role or recent post when possible.
- Signature: just your name + abol.ai. No corporate fluff.

**Sending cadence:**
- Monday + Wednesday mornings, 09:00-10:30 CET. EU execs open LinkedIn with their coffee.
- Variant A is the safe default. Variant B is for when Mythos/agentic AI is fresh in the news. Variant C is for people you suspect lead with curiosity, not compliance.
- Max 20 DMs/day to avoid LinkedIn throttling.
- Follow-up after 5 business days (see Follow-up section below).

Skip it. Two touches, then leave them alone. Don't be that person.

## When someone responds

**If they ran the scan:**
Ask one specific question about which pillar surprised them. That's the honest conversation. If they scored Fair or below on compliance: "Would the €425 full report with the top 10 prioritized actions be useful for your board cycle, or is a 30-min call faster?"

**If they didn't run it but replied:**
Don't push the scan again. Ask what they're actually trying to solve this quarter. The benchmark may not be the answer today. A real conversation is worth more than another DM.

**If they say "we already know our posture":**
"That's fair. The 9-min scan runs against Thales/IBM/ENISA/Verizon DBIR peer medians, which most internal assessments don't include. If your board asks for a peer comparison citation, here's where we get ours: abol.ai/methodology. Zero pressure to run it."

---

## What NOT to say

- "I'd love to jump on a 15-min call." Nobody has 15 minutes. Link to a 9-minute self-serve scan instead.
- "Just checking in." Zero information content. Say what you want.
- "This will revolutionize your..." No.
- Any mention of "AI-powered" or "next-gen" without a specific quantity. Dry register only.
- "Hope this finds you well."
