Your score sits next to two reference lines for your sector:

- **Peer median** — the middle of the distribution

Both values are sourced from four public benchmarks, cited on every chart:

| Source | Sample size | Why it's here |

This is the cold-start dataset. As scans accumulate, proprietary cohort data blends in. Sources remain cited on every chart.
